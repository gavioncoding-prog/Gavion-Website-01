## Pedagogy meets infrastructure

We designed infrastructure that flexes with curriculum — not the other way around. Power, data, and dust collection can be re-homed as programs evolve.

## Inclusion

Ramp gradients, tactile paths, and assistive listening loops exceed minimum compliance — because dignity should be engineered in, not retrofitted.

## Operating constraints

Expanding an active hospital is an exercise in choreography. Our construction methodology prioritized vibration monitoring near imaging suites and maintained sterile corridors via negative-pressure tunnels.

## Engineering highlights

Steel transfer levels were detailed for crane reach limits along the Brahmaputra floodplain approach roads. We sequenced concrete pours to align with monsoon windows, protecting schedule integrity.

## Legacy

The expansion demonstrates how disciplined logistics and transparent reporting keep healing environments stable — even when steel is flying overhead.

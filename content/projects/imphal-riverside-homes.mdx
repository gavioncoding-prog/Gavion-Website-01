## Design philosophy

We merged contemporary space planning with Manipuri courtyard traditions — creating shade, ventilation, and social cohesion without sacrificing density targets.

## Flood readiness

Mechanical and electrical backbone routes above design flood levels. Backup power for common pumps ensures safe egress lighting when it matters most.

## Technology layer

IoT-enabled leak detection in podiums gives facility managers predictive alerts, reducing insurance exposure for homeowners associations.

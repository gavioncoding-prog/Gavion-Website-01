## Systems thinking

Agriculture projects are civil + digital + social. We engineered civil channels for predictable flow, then layered telemetry so cooperatives could schedule irrigation like airlines schedule gates.

## Resilience

Flood-season protocols reroute control signals through mesh relays when fiber backhaul is interrupted — keeping pumps from running blind.

## Scale pathway

The corridor blueprint is modular: new nodes snap into the same SCADA grammar, reducing marginal cost for future districts.

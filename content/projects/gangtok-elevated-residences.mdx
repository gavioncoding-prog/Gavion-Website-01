## Site intelligence

Steep topography required creative massing. We paired geotechnical telemetry with conservative bearing assumptions, then validated every lift pour against slope stability models.

## Resident experience

Interiors receive consistent daylight without glare penalties common in mountain towers. Acoustic isolation between units exceeds NBC guidance — a quiet promise for families.

## Sustainability

On-site solar skirting and centralized hot-water preheat trim carbon intensity while respecting the municipality’s aesthetic codes.

## Context

The Eastern Himalayas demand structures that respect both seismic energy and extreme seasonal rainfall. Gavion Group led integrated structural, MEP coordination, and construction sequencing for this greenfield hospital — aligning India’s clinical standards with constructible detailing suited to highland logistics.

## Delivery approach

We deployed digital layout validation before slab pours, reducing rework on penetrations and medical gas risers. Field teams used a single source of truth for QA checklists, ensuring infection-control boundaries were never compromised during phased handover.

## Outcomes

The institute now operates with redundant power paths, a resilient envelope, and expansion sleeves for future bed towers — proof that precision engineering and compassionate healthcare can share the same foundation.

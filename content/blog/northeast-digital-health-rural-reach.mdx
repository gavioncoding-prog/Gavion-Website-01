## What “digital” actually buys in a district hospital

1. **Triage discipline** — structured data at first touch, even on paper-backed workflows.
2. **Radiology & pathology bridges** — images and reports that do not need a night bus to Guwahati.
3. **Credentialled follow-up** — HWC teams acting on the same problem list as the tertiary centre.

<KeyTakeaways title="Where to invest next (North-East lens)">

- **Last-mile power** — online UPS sizing for telecart endpoints, not only the server room.
- **Linguistic UX** — Roman script alone often fails; plan for **local language** labels on patient-facing kiosks.
- **Post-earthquake cable trays** — digital health dies when **structured cabling** pulls out of gland plates.

</KeyTakeaways>

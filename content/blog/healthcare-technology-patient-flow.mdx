## Start with the journey map

Technology procurement often begins with vendor demos. We begin with **nurse steps per shift** and **equipment utilization curves**. The delta is where budget should land.

## Integration principles

- **Fewer dashboards, deeper signals** — alarm fatigue kills trust faster than any outage.
- **Cyber hygiene by design** — VLAN segmentation for clinical devices is not optional.
- **Future-proof pathways** — empty conduits are cheaper than core drilling occupied ICUs.

## Construction handover

Commissioning is where hospitals win or lose a decade. We script integrated testing with clinical stakeholders in the room — not two months after opening.

## Bottom line

Great healthcare buildings feel calm. Technology should amplify that calm, not compete with it.

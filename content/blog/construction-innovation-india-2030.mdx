## Hype versus habit

Innovation that survives on site shares three traits: **trainable**, **inspectable**, and **insurable**. Everything else is a pilot deck.

## What we are investing in

- **Industrialized bathroom pods** for hospitality and healthcare — when QC moves to the factory, field variance drops.
- **Computer vision for safety** — not to replace walks, but to catch blind spots at dusk.
- **Structured data handover** — so FM teams inherit models they can actually maintain.

## What we are cautious about

Fully autonomous layout robots sound brilliant until a monsoon shifts your control network. We prefer augmentation: humans plus machines, with explicit fail-safes.

## For public clients

Pilot clauses belong in contracts — with measurable acceptance tests and off-ramps. Innovation without governance becomes expensive theater.

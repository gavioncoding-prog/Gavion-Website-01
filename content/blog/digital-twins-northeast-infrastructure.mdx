## The case for a single source of truth

In the North-East, projects contend with aggressive monsoons, steep logistics, and multi-agency stakeholders. A digital twin is not a render — it is an operational spine that connects **as-built geometry**, **sensor streams**, and **maintenance history**.

## Where twins earn their keep

1. **Predictive facilities** — correlate humidity spikes with envelope leaks before mold becomes a retrofit.
2. **Capital planning** — simulate elevator modernization impacts on hospital traffic before committing steel.
3. **Emergency rehearsal** — run evacuation scenarios against live occupancy estimates.

## Practical adoption

Start narrow: pick one subsystem (power, HVAC, or vertical transport), instrument it honestly, and prove ROI in twelve months. Scale only when field teams trust the dashboard more than the group chat.

> The best twin is the one foremen open without being asked.

## Closing thought

Gavion Group treats technology as civil infrastructure — invisible when perfect, unforgettable when missing. Twins are how we keep it perfect longer.
